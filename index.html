Common Questions

Good to know.

How much does interior design cost?

Malaysia: Apartment/condo renovations typically range from RM50,000–RM150,000, terrace houses from RM80,000–RM250,000, semi-D from RM150,000–RM400,000, and bungalows from RM250,000–RM800,000+.

Singapore: HDB/BTO renovations range from $25,000–$70,000, condominiums from $50,000–$150,000, and landed properties from $100,000–$500,000+.

We provide transparent, itemized quotations after a complimentary site consultation. No hidden fees.

How long does a renovation take?

A typical apartment or condominium renovation takes 8–12 weeks from design confirmation to handover. Terrace house projects take 10–16 weeks, and semi-detached or bungalow homes 16–24 weeks. This includes design development, permit approvals, and construction.

Do I need a licensed contractor?

Yes. In Malaysia, you need a CIDB G2-registered contractor for structural, electrical, or plumbing work. In Singapore, HDB renovations require a BCA-licensed contractor. Studio FPI works exclusively with licensed contractors in both countries and handles all permit applications on your behalf.

What does your design process look like?

We follow a 4-phase process: (1) Discovery, a free consultation to understand your lifestyle; (2) Material Story, curating your textures and finishes; (3) Spatial Craft, 3D renders and detailed planning; (4) Realization, construction with weekly updates and full project management.

Do you handle renovation permits and approvals?

Yes. In Malaysia, we handle all PBT permits, CIDB submissions, and local council approvals. In Singapore, we manage HDB renovation permits, BCA submissions, and town council approvals. For condominiums in both countries, we coordinate with building management.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes. The first consultation is always complimentary and no-obligation. We'll visit your space, discuss your vision, and provide a preliminary scope and budget estimate within 5 working days.
